## йҳҝзӯ– (Ace / Strategist)
- **Role**: Likely a Strategist or Planning persona. 
- **Focus**: High-level strategy, architectural decisions, and task orchestration.
- **Context**: Often used when the system needs to switch from execution (doing) to strategy (planning).

## йҒ”зҲҫ (XiaoCai)
- **Role**: The primary AI Task Executor / Assistant.
- **Focus**: Executing bash scripts, managing the workspace, and fulfilling direct user requests.
- **Identity**: Built on the OpenClaw framework.

## Key Differences
1. **Execution vs. Strategy**: йҒ”зҲҫ handles the "how" (implementation), while йҳҝзӯ– handles the "what/why" (strategic direction).
2. **Persistence**: йҒ”зҲҫ's role is embedded in the environment scripts; йҳҝзӯ– may be an emergent persona for complex reasoning tasks.
